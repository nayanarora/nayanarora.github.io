About

Software Developer Dedicated to Innovative Technological Advances.

I am Nayan, a versatile software developer with a passion for leveraging latest technology to create impactful solutions. With a primary background in software development, Data Management, AI/ML, and Reseach Analysis, I have consistently delivered innovative results across diverse domains. I hold a Master's in Information Technology, Majors in Artifical Intelligence and Machine Learning from the University of Canberra and a Bachelor's in Computer Science from Arizona State University.

Throughout my career, I have worked on a variety of projects, from designing immersive VR/AR experiences to developing advanced prediction models using LSTM, CNN, Random Forest, XGBoost as well as various transfer learning models including YOLOv8 for object detection and Inception v3 for image classification. My experience spans multiple programming languages and technologies, including Python, TensorFlow, C++, C#, Unity3D, and more. I thrive in dynamic environments, where I can apply my technical expertise and strategic thinking to drive results.

I am passionate about contributing to advancements in safety, innovation and inclusivitiy in technology and I am dedicated to continuous learning and professional growth.
